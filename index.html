The Journey

The Five Elements.
Your Map.

The alchemy of the human being isn't metaphor, it's a precise system. Each of the five elements corresponds to a layer of your body, your psychology, and your life force. We work through them deliberately, in sequence, from the most foundational to the most expansive.

  1. 🜃

    Earth

    Stability · Bones · Legs · Foundations · Energy · Resources

    We begin here, always. Before transformation comes ground. This layer addresses your foundational needs, your safety in the body, the roots from which everything else grows. We work with the bones, the legs, the structural self.

  2. 🜄

    Water

    Flow · Hips · Psoas · Emotional Body · Digestion

    What can't be felt gets stored. Water moves what has been held, emotional blockages, old grief, the tight places in the fascia and belly. This is the layer of release, of restored flow, of meeting what has been waiting to be felt.

  3. 🜂

    Fire

    Expression · Chest · Breath · Lungs · Passion

    Your life force expression lives here. When Fire is suppressed, you feel flat, uninspired, going through motions. We reignite the breath, free the chest, move what's stuck, reconnect you to what you actually want, not what you've settled for.

  4. 🜁

    Air

    Clarity · Nervous System · Head · Thought Patterns

    The mind is a portal, or a cage, depending on what runs it. This layer clears mental noise, rewires looping patterns, and restores the nervous system's capacity to be present and regulated. We move from overthinking into knowing.

  5. Ether

    Connection · Energetic Body · Intuition · Infinite Potential

    The fifth element is where you touch something larger than the personal story. Your creative intelligence, your intuition, your capacity for genuine vision, these live here. We don't work up to this layer. It opens when you are ready to receive.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How is this different from therapy?

Therapy works primarily through language and cognitive understanding. This work enters through the body first, sensation, breath, movement, energy. The two can be complementary; they are not the same thing.

Do I need to believe in this for it to work?

No. You need to be curious and willing. The body responds to the work regardless of belief systems. What matters is your genuine desire to change.

How many sessions does the journey take?

The 1:1 Alchemical Journey is a minimum 3-month container. The depth of this work needs time. We discuss the specifics on your discovery call.

Is this in person or online?

Both. Sessions are available in person or via video call. Some somatic practices are more effective in person; we adapt to what's possible for you.

I've never done somatic or energy work before, is that okay?

Yes. Many clients come without prior experience. What matters is willingness to be in your body. I'll guide everything.

Is this a substitute for therapy or medical care?

No. This work is a complementary practice, not a replacement for medical or psychological treatment. If you're navigating a health condition, please also work with a licensed professional. Full details are in our disclaimer below.
